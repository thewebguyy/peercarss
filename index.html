  • Peercars have partnered with a good insurance company to ensure total coverage on every rental.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is peer to peer car rental?

    With peer-to-peer car sharing, car owners are able to make money by renting out their vehicle when they are not using it. Renters can access nearby and affordable vehicles and pay only for the time they need to use them. Car owners list their cars on the platform, specifying everything from car’s make, model, color, and transmission. Prospective renters can look up these cars and rent out the one that matches their requirements the best. The cars can be rented out for anywhere from a few hours, a day, or a week.

  • What do I need to book a car?

    To book a car you must be 18 years old or older with a valid driver’s license, valid address.

  • What if I have an accident?

    If there’s an accident call Peercar’s emergency number to inform us about the situation.
